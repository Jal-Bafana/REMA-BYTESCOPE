# Presentation Guide

## Recommended Demo Sequence

1. Open `index.html` and introduce ByteScope as a realistic blog.
2. Open one article to show editorial depth.
3. In `article-secure-prompting.html`, click sponsored offer.
4. Complete sign-in on `login.html`.
5. Click coupon on `dashboard.html`.
6. Trigger download on `malicious.html`.
7. Open `defense-site/index.html` in a separate tab.
8. Paste downloaded TXT content.
9. Click decode and explain extracted and decoded views.

## Suggested Talking Points

- The lure appears in context, not on homepage.
- Multi-step interaction builds user trust.
- Downloaded artifact is text and safe to inspect.
- Defender workflow is intentionally separated from public site.
- Decode process reveals suspicious intent patterns.
